Homemade Hemp Milk

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of raw unshelled hemp seeds
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1-2 tsp Maple syrup
  • 1/4 tsp of salt

Directions

  1. Add all the ingredients to a high speed blender
    1. Plug blender in
    2. Turn blender on high-speed setting
  2. Blend on high for about 2 minutes
  3. Store in fridge for up to 5 days
    4. If you let it sit for more than 5 days it will go bad.
